Joining a Video Room

1

Get the Room Link

Access Videorama from: The PNPtv bot menu ‚Üí Videorama section, or directly from pnptv.app/videorama, or community group pinned messages.

2

Accept Adult Content Warning

First time joining requires 18+ confirmation. Click "I Agree - Enter" to proceed. This is saved for your session.

3

Choose how you want to appear to others. You can use your real name, username, or stay anonymous. Can be changed anytime.

4

Allow Camera & Microphone

Your browser will ask for permissions. Click "Allow" to enable video and audio. You can disable them later with the room controls.

5

You'll see other participants' video feeds. Your video appears in the corner. Use the bottom toolbar to control camera, mic, screen share, and more.

  • Test first: Check your camera/mic before joining a busy room
  • Be respectful: Follow community guidelines at all times
