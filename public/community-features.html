‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Q

How do I access the bot menu?

Simply type /menu in your chat with @PNPtvbot. The menu shows all available features organized by category. You can navigate using the inline buttons.

Q

What is Cristina AI Assistant?

Cristina is our AI-powered assistant that can answer questions, help you navigate, and provide instant support. Type /cristina in the bot to start chatting with her anytime.

Q

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can manage your subscription through the bot menu (Subscription & Access ‚Üí Subscription Status). Note: Lifetime Pass is non-refundable but never expires.

Q

Is my payment information safe?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. We never store your card details. All transactions are encrypted end-to-end.

Q

What devices can I use?

Everything works on desktop, laptop, tablet, and mobile. The bot works on all Telegram platforms. Video rooms work best on desktop/laptop but are mobile-compatible.

Q

Can I use multiple devices?

Yes! Your subscription works on unlimited devices. Just log into Telegram on each device. However, you can't be in multiple video rooms simultaneously.

Q

How do I get support?

Message @PNPtvbot anytime for 24/7 support. Type /menu ‚Üí Support & Help, or just ask your question directly. You can also use /cristina for AI assistance.

Q

What's the difference between PRIME and Community Group?

PRIME is a broadcast channel where admins post exclusive content (view-only). Community Group is for member discussions and socializing. Both are included with your subscription.
