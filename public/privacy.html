1 Overview

PNPtv! Digital Community ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Our Core Principle: We collect only what's necessary to provide our services and protect our community. We never sell your personal data.

2 Data We Collect

What We Collect
  • Telegram user ID and handle
  • Email (if provided for payments)
  • Subscription status and history
  • Age verification result (yes/no)
  • Optional: Approximate location (if you enable geolocation)
  • Support communications
What We DON'T Collect
  • Facial images or photos (deleted immediately)
  • Biometric identifiers
  • Precise GPS location
  • Browsing history outside PNPtv
  • Contact lists
  • Private messages on Telegram

3 Age Verification & Face++

We use Face++ AI technology for optional camera-based age verification. Your privacy is our top priority during this process.

How It Works

  1. You take a selfie using your device camera
  2. The photo is sent securely (HTTPS) to Face++ AI for age estimation
  3. Face++ returns only an estimated age number
  4. Your photo is deleted immediately - we never store it
What We Store
  • Verification date
  • Result (verified yes/no)
  • Estimated age range
  • Verification method used
What We NEVER Store
  • Facial photographs
  • Facial feature data
  • Biometric templates
  • Facial recognition identifiers

Alternative Available: You can always choose manual age confirmation without providing any photo. This is a self-declaration method.

Face++ privacy policy: faceplusplus.com/privacy

4 How We Use Your Data

Promotional Messages

5 Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or trade your personal data. Period.

We may share limited data with:

  • Payment Processors: ePayco and Daimo for transaction processing only
  • Age Verification: Face++ receives only the photo (immediately deleted)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Service Providers: Hosting and infrastructure providers under strict contracts

6 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage

  • Access controls and authentication

  • DDoS protection

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and protect your account credentials.
