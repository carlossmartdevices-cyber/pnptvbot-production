### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Can't Access Content

**Problem:** Subscription active but can't view content

**Solutions:**
1. Verify subscription status: `/menu ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Status`
2. Check expiration date
3. Restart Telegram app
4. Re-join group (if applicable)
5. Clear app cache
6. Contact support if persists

#### Payment Not Processing

**Problem:** Payment declined or stuck

**Solutions:**
1. Verify card details correct
2. Check bank balance sufficient
3. Ensure card supports international transactions
4. Try different payment method
5. Contact your bank
6. Use `/cristina payment help`

#### Not Receiving Notifications

**Problem:** Missing important notifications

**Solutions:**
1. Check notification settings: `/menu ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Notifications`
2. Verify Telegram notification permissions
3. Check phone's Do Not Disturb mode
4. Ensure bot not muted
5. Check spam/archived chats
6. Re-enable notifications

#### Can't Join Group

**Problem:** Unable to access member group

**Solutions:**
1. Verify subscription active
2. Check username compliance: `/menu ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Profile`
3. Accept group rules
4. Request new invitation link
5. Check if banned (contact support)
6. Verify Telegram account age (7+ days)

#### Bot Not Responding

**Problem:** Commands not working

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Restart Telegram app
3. Clear chat history
4. Wait 5 minutes (may be updating)
5. Try `/start` command
6. Contact support if continues

#### Wrong Language Displayed

**Problem:** Bot showing wrong language

**Solutions:**
1. Change manually: `/menu ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Language`
2. Check Telegram language setting
3. Restart conversation with `/start`
4. Clear cache
5. Contact support

#### Content Won't Load

**Problem:** Videos/images not displaying

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet speed
2. Switch to WiFi if on mobile data
3. Clear Telegram cache
4. Update Telegram to latest version
5. Try different device
6. Download instead of streaming

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is my subscription recurring?**
A: Yes, by default subscriptions auto-renew. You can disable this in payment settings.

**Q: Can I share my account?**
A: No, subscriptions are personal and non-transferable. Account sharing may result in termination.

**Q: What happens if I cancel?**
A: You'll have access until your current period ends. No partial refunds except within 30-day window.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Not currently, but you can cancel and re-subscribe later without losing your membership history.

**Q: Do you offer student discounts?**
A: Contact support with proof of enrollment for potential discount.

### Content Questions

**Q: How often is new content added?**
A: Daily photos, weekly videos, weekly live streams, and weekly podcasts.

**Q: Can I download content?**
A: Yes, most content is downloadable for offline viewing (personal use only).

**Q: How long do I have access to past content?**
A: As long as your subscription is active, you have access to all archived content.

**Q: Can I request specific content?**
A: Yes! Use `/cristina content request [description]` or participate in polls.

**Q: Is content age-restricted?**
A: Some content may be age-restricted. You must be 18+ for full access.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which devices are supported?**
A: Any device that runs Telegram (iOS, Android, Desktop, Web).

**Q: Do I need a Telegram Premium account?**
A: No, standard Telegram is sufficient.

**Q: What internet speed do I need?**
A: Minimum 3 Mbps for streaming, 5 Mbps recommended for HD.

**Q: Can I use VPN?**
A: Yes, VPN use is allowed.

**Q: What if the bot is down?**
A: Check announcements in group. Downtime is rare and usually brief for updates.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Are payments secure?**
A: Yes, we use PCI-compliant payment processors with 256-bit encryption.

**Q: Do you store my card details?**
A: No, payment details are tokenized and stored by our payment processor, not us.

**Q: Can I get a receipt?**
A: Yes, automatic email receipts and downloadable invoices available in payment history.

**Q: What currency do you accept?**
A: USD, EUR, GBP, and cryptocurrency.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No, the price you see is the price you pay (plus applicable taxes).

### Community Questions

**Q: Can I invite friends?**
A: Yes! Use referral system (coming soon) for rewards.

**Q: How do I become VIP?**
A: Upgrade your badge in Community settings or earn through activity.

**Q: Can I be a moderator?**
A: Active members may be invited to join moderation team.

**Q: What if I'm harassed?**
A: Report immediately via `/cristina report [username]`. Zero tolerance policy.

**Q: Can I create a topic?**
A: Only admins can create topics, but you can suggest topics via support.

### Account Questions

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: Yes, change in Telegram settings. Allow 10 minutes for bot to update.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support to request account deletion (subscription will be canceled).

**Q: Can I transfer my subscription?**
A: No, subscriptions are non-transferable.

**Q: What if I forgot which email I used?**
A: Contact support with your Telegram username for assistance.

**Q: How do I recover a banned account?**
A: Submit appeal via support email with explanation and username.
